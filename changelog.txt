Version: 1.1.0
Date: 07. 01. 2026
  Modding:
    - Added support for other planet mods
    - planet mods wanting to proactively integrate with us can do so using remote "wormholes-new-game-plus"."register_planet"
    - unregistered planets are skipped
    - registered planets we know will not crash the game are reset as before
    - registered planets we know WILL crash the game if we reset them are skipped.
    - messages in chat point the player to update their mods and check discussions for if such planets are detected.
    - "Rubia" and "Maraxsis" have both been flagged as unsupported for now, possible support in future.
  Features:
    - all space platforms at the wormhole are teleported to a random planet supported by the mod. Largely a convenience feature.
